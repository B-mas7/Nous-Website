About Novus Path Labs

Novus Pathlabs is a Medical Laboratory where all especial test and Routine Test are Done.

Established in 2073 B.S., Novus Path Labs is a modern diagnostic centre located in Lampati, Kalanki, dedicated to providing accurate, affordable, and accessible laboratory services. + We are NPHL-certified under B Category. ensuring our laboratory practices meet national standards in quality and reliability. Our expert pathologists and trained staff use state-of-the-art equipment to deliver dependable reports with precision. Whether its a basic test or an advanced health screening package, our commit-ment is to help you take control of your health with confidence.

KUBER DAHA

Founder & CEO

Kuber Dahal is a renowned pathologist with over 20 years of experience in medical diagnostics. He completed his MD in Pathology from AIIMS Delhi and has worked with several prestigious hospitals before establishing Novus Path Labs.

Under his leadership, Novus Path Labs has grown to become one of the most trusted diagnostic centers in the region, known for its accuracy, reliability, and patient-centric approach.

Kuber Dahal is passionate about incorporating the latest technological advancements in laboratory medicine to ensure the highest quality of diagnostic services for patients.

"Our mission at Novus Path Labs is not just to provide test results, but to contribute meaningfully to the healthcare journey of every patient who walks through our doors. Accuracy in diagnostics can save lives, and we take that responsibility very seriously."

- Kuber Dahal
